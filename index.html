About DWRA

Providing access to healthcare for children who need it most.

Diaspora Works and Relief Agency (DWRA Foundation) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting children by helping provide access to health insurance and essential medical care.

Through the generosity of donors, we are working to remove barriers to treatment, protect children from preventable illness, and create a healthier future for the next generation.

Who we are

Diaspora Works and Relief Agency (DWRA Foundation) is a nonprofit organization in the United States supporting children by helping provide free health insurance and better access to medical care.

The goal is to protect children from preventable illness, reduce barriers to treatment, and create a stronger foundation for long-term health and well-being.

Our mission

  • Provide access to health coverage for children in need
  • Support medical treatment and essential care
  • Protect children from preventable illness
  • Create opportunities for healthier futures
  • Build a community of support through generosity
